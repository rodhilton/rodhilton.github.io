Once upon a time, I participated in a large number of private torrent tracker sites. One thing I always heard was: "how can I increase my ratio?"

If you are a member of a private tracker site, you know that one of the hardest things to do is increase your ratio easily. Many private trackers ban you for dropping below a specific ratio, but very often people find that when they download a torrent, nobody else seems to request those torrents afterwards, leaving the ratio of that torrent at 0.0 (you can't upload what nobody else wants).

Obviously, the best way to increase your ratio is to upload, but most people who are new to a private tracker aren't comfortable uploading, or find that they have no content that they can contribute. So the question remains: "how do I increase my ratio without uploading something new?"
